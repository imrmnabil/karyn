The Science

The Evidence Behind Hypnotherapy for Menopause

Clinical research has shown that hypnotherapy can be an effective, natural approach for managing menopausal symptoms such as hot flushes, sleep disturbance, and stress. In a randomized clinical trial published in the Menopause journal, women receiving hypnotherapy experienced reductions in hot flashes of up to 74%. Other studies have found that hypnosis significantly reduces both the frequency and severity of hot flashes, while also improving sleep quality and overall wellbeing.

For women seeking a non-hormonal, drug-free option, hypnotherapy is increasingly recognised as a supportive and evidence-based approach.
